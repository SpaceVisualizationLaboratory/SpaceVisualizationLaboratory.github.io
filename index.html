    The Space Visualization Laboratory looks to create innovative experiences that educate the public about the wonders of our Universe. Here are some featured projects.

    A BYOD planetarium captioning system

    BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) captioning system for planetaria, as implemented by the Adler Planetarium here. Timed captions are fed to the users mobile device. There are two versions of the code, one where the timing of the caption stream is triggered by the user AdlerSubtitlesManual, and another where the show operator can syncronise the playing back of the captioning AdlerSubtitles
