    Project maintained by SpaceVisualizationLaboratory

    The Space Visualization Laboratory looks to create innovative experiences that educate the public about the wonders of our Universe. Here are some of our featured projects:

    A BYOD planetarium captioning system

    BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) captioning system for planetaria, as implemented by the Adler Planetarium here. Timed captions are fed to the user's mobile device. There are two versions of the code, one where the timing of the caption stream is triggered by the user AdlerSubtitlesManual, and another where the show operator can syncronise the playing back of the caption stream AdlerSubtitles

    The Gravitational Simulator

    A Kinect based gravity simulation program. Visitors use their bodies to create artifical stars in the simulation. GravitationalSimulator

    The Smart Window

    An exhibit piece utalizing switchable glass, a Raspberry Pi, an IR motion detector and an ultra-short through projector SmartWindow

    The SVL's WWT based TileWall

    Resources associated with the SVL's 15 panel Worldwide Telescope TimeWall with Kinect and Midi interfaces WWT_TileWall
